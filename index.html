About Us


Although our business has evolved over the years, one constant is customers desire for lower prices, better selection, and convenient services. Today, Redstore shoppers can find what they are looking for online and in person. We operate a lot like a set of startups, embracing invention and creating stores, devices, and services that our customers will use, share, and love.

Our passion for delighting customers drives us to constantly invent on their behalf. Working backward from customer needs, we’re constantly seeking to improve our services, add benefits and features, and invent new products.
